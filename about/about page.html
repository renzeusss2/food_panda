About FoodPanda

Your favorite meals delivered fast & fresh.

About Us

FoodPanda is a leading online food delivery platform that connects hungry customers with their favorite restaurants. Founded in 2012 in Berlin, Germany, we have rapidly expanded to serve millions of users across Asia, Europe, and beyond, making it easy to order delicious meals from the comfort of your home or office.

Our platform offers a wide variety of cuisines, from local favorites to international delights. With a focus on convenience, speed, and quality, FoodPanda is your go-to partner for satisfying cravings. We partner with over 100,000 restaurants and employ thousands of delivery partners to bring food to your doorstep.

Our History

FoodPanda was launched in 2012 by a team of entrepreneurs who saw the potential in revolutionizing food delivery. Starting in Singapore, we quickly expanded across Asia.

In 2016, we joined Delivery Hero, a global leader in online food ordering, accelerating our reach into over 40 countries worldwide.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the way people enjoy food by providing seamless and enjoyable delivery experiences. We strive to support local restaurants, empower delivery partners, and delight customers with every order.

We are committed to sustainability, innovation, and community building—making food delivery not just a service, but a positive part of everyday life.

Our Services

  • Food Delivery: Fast, reliable, real-time tracked delivery.
  • Restaurant Partnerships: Helping businesses expand in the digital age.
  • Delivery Hero Ecosystem: Groceries and essentials delivery integration.
  • Customer Support: 24/7 assistance for all your needs.
  • Special Features: PandaPoints, group orders, promos & more.

Our Impact

FoodPanda has created thousands of jobs, boosted restaurant sales, and revolutionized access to food across the globe. With over 1 billion orders processed, we continue to innovate for a better and more sustainable future.

Our community and social programs include food donation partnerships, eco-friendly packaging, and green delivery options.
